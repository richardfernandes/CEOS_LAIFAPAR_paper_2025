some columns you may be curious :

t_x,t_y, UTM projection coordinates of GEDI footprint

match_x, match_y: UTM projection coordinates after footprint offset adjustment

distance_offset: geolocation offset distance

NLCD: land cover type derived from National Land Cover Database (2019)

CHM_98: canopy height at the GEDI reported location

CHM_98_c: canopy height at the adjusted GEDI location

rhReal98, ALScover_s, cover_s, beamDense_s,zenith_s,groundslope_s, layered_LAI_t: derived from gediSimulator: https://bitbucket.org/StevenHancock/gedisimulator/src/master/

slope: terrain slope on the adjusted GEDI location

SCI: reference  canopy cover derived from point cloud at the GEDI reported location

SCI_c: canopy cover at the adjusted GEDI location

PAI_NEON: reference PAI directly estimated by -ln(1 - SCI_c)/0.5
